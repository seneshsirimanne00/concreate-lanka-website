Products Overview

Our spacers fulfill the requirements of all exposure classes. All of our mix designs used are tested by independent testing institute with regards to the required properties. Our products include 15mm to 150mm Slab Spacers, Bar Spacers 200mm to 450mm, Circular Spacers and Panel Control Spacers our products comply with BS7973 standard.
